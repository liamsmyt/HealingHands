I am delighted to introduce a new programme at Healing Hands - the Complete Holistic Fertility Package, designed for couples navigating the subfertility journey together.

Subfertility brings medical, emotional, and spiritual challenges that can feel isolating. This package offers a whole-istic approach - combining evidence-based complementary therapies with compassionate, couple-centred care to support physical wellness, emotional balance, and connection between partners.

What's included?
  • Fertility reflexology - a minimum of 8 individual weekly sessions per partner, tailored to stimulate reproductive reflex points, balance hormones, and reduce stress
  • Sound therapy - shared sessions using singing bowls, gongs, drums, and more to ease tension and support emotional well-being
  • Bioenergy & tuning forks - working with the sacral and heart chakras to support energy flow, circulation, and connection between partners

Important: This programme is designed for couples, and both partners must be present for all elements - including induction, bio-energy, sound therapy, and reflexology sessions.

Many clients describe feeling more grounded, open, and aligned after sessions - creating a nurturing space for conception and emotional well-being. Protocols are reviewed weekly and adjusted to suit each couple's needs.
