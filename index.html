Reflexology

Reflexology is a holistic practice that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet and hands, which correspond to parts of the body. This technique helps improve circulation, promote relaxation, and restore the body's balance (homeostasis). It activates the body's own healing processes and supports overall well-being.

Hopi-Ear Candling

The therapy involves inserting a special beeswax candle into the ear, releasing herbal vapors that massage the ear canal and soften impurities. Sound Therapy

Sound therapy, an ancient practice from Tibetan, Native American, Egyptian, and Indian cultures, uses sound to promote healing. Based on the idea that everything vibrates at a unique frequency, it helps restore balance when the body is out of harmony, improving overall health and well-being as well as evoking healing on physical, mental, and spiritual levels.
