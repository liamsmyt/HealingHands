Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Lymphatic drainage massage is a slow, rhythmic massage, which follows the path of the lymphatic system of the body. Reflexology Lymph Drainage (RLD) is a specific reflexology technique which focuses on stimulating the lymphatic reflexes on the feet.
