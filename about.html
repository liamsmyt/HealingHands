Bernadette Doyle: Sound Therapy Specialist

Healing Hands Health and Wellness, Newbridge

Bernadette Doyle is a dedicated sound therapist based at Healing Hands Health and Wellness in Newbridge. As a member of the Association of Sound Therapists Ireland (AOSTI), Bernadette brings professional expertise and a compassionate approach to her practice, focusing exclusively on the transformative power of sound therapy.

Bernadette specialises in offering one-to-one, personalised sound treatments at her clinic, tailoring each session to the unique needs of her clients. Her sound therapy services are designed to promote relaxation, emotional balance, and holistic wellbeing.

In addition to individual sessions, Bernadette is actively involved in corporate wellness initiatives, providing sound therapy experiences during wellness days to help employees manage stress and enhance workplace wellbeing. She also curates special packages for couples and delivers bespoke sound therapy sessions for special events, ensuring memorable and meaningful experiences for all participants.
