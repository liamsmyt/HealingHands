How does Hot Stone Massage work?

Placing hot stones on the body and arranging them along its energy centres helps to relax the muscles, reduces pain or discomfort and boosts the healing process. The massage following the placing of the stones can be deeper and more intense as the heat will have penetrated the body and relaxed the muscles.

Conditions that benefit from hot stone massage

  • Muscular aches, pains and strains
  • Rheumatic and arthritic conditions
  • Muscular sclerosis
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Insomnia
  • Poor circulation
  • Stress, tension
  • Depression and anxiety
